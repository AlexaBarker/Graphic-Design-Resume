My name is Alexa Barker and I have practice in international policy and graphic design. I have gained a range of experience from both my time at Centre College and various internships including the Kentucky Office of Homeland Security and the U.S State Department.

Outside of graphic design I have experience in defense policy research, professional writing, policy memos, and regional conflict deterrence strategy. I feel at home in a team environment, and look forward to new collaborations.
